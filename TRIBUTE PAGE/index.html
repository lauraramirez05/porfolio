Accomplishments

Karol G has become a real hit for reggaeton and the urban genre, breaking many records in the music industry.

  • Karol G broke a record on Spotify for the biggest debit in history for a Spanish album by a Latina artist
  • "Tusa" is the first Latin song by a woman to reach 1 billion streams on Spotify.
  • "KG0516" claimed No. 1 on Billboard's Top Latin Albums chart. It dethroned Bad Bunny's 2020 full-length "El Ultimo Tour del Mundo" album.
  • First Woman to win "Artist of the Year" at Latin American Music Awards.

Importance

Karol G is a Colombian singer. She is an amazing representation for the Latino community and women. She has become an icon of female empowerment in a genre that has been dominated by men. She honors her roots in important concerts by rendering iconic tributes to Latin musicians.
